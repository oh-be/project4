Property Investment Calculator


There are plenty of benefits of investing in property. There are great longterm returns, it is a relativelyl safe and stable investment, it provides passive income, and so much more.


However, most people are not trained professionals in the field and therefore do not know what properities would be a smart purchase or a good longterm investment.


With this calculator, anyone would be able to upload information about a property they are considering buying and find out whether or not it is a smart investment.
